How It Works

A structured rhythm. Not a course. Not a mastermind.

Before

Pre-Work

Arrives 48 to 72 hours before each session. 20 minutes. Surfaces the raw material the session needs. The more honestly you answer, the faster the session moves.

During

Live Session

90 minutes on Zoom. Teaching, silent worksheet work, and two live hot seats where participants work through their material directly with the coach.

After

Commitment Close

A post session form locks your key output, one decision, and one action with a deadline. What gets written gets done.

Between

Real World Action

One specific action within 72 hours. Slack for accountability, wins, and clarifying questions between sessions.

Jimmy Clarke

Brand Strategist  ·  Leadership Coach  ·  Founder

I did not come to this work from a certification. I came from 30 years of building things at the highest levels: teams, strategies, companies, and the clarity that only comes from making consequential decisions under real pressure.

From Fortune 500 boardrooms to small business owners, I have spent 20 years helping brands find their voice and reach the right people online. As founder and CEO of XPND Interactive for 13 years, I lived every positioning, identity, and direction decision this program addresses. Not in theory. In practice, with real stakes.

I spent nearly a decade as a senior sales leader at CBS Radio and Entercom, directing $40 million in revenue across six stations and building teams from the ground up. Before that, I competed professionally as a soccer player for the Portland Pride.

I built Get Unstuck because I kept seeing the same pattern in the wellness and coaching world: practitioners with real gifts, stuck on the same clarity problem. Not because they lacked skill, but because no one had given them a rigorous process for building their foundation from identity outward. This program is that process.
