A Letter to
Your Future Self

Written today. Opened in 90 days.

This letter is written in your own words in the final moments of Session 6. It is not a worksheet. It is not a template. It is a message from the person you are right now to the person you will be in 90 days. Write it as if you are speaking to someone you care about who needs to remember what was decided in this room today.

01
Section 1 Who I Am Now

Not a summary of the program. A statement of identity. Who are you now that you were not before? What do you know about yourself that changes the way you operate?

02
Section 2 The Pattern I Am Watching

Named and witnessed in the room today. Write the pattern by name. Write when it shows up. Write what it sounds like when it speaks. And write what you will say back to it when it does.

03
Section 3 My Three 90-Day Priorities

Write each priority as a leadership statement. Not a task. A decision about how you will operate. These should be recognizable to you in 90 days as the commitments you made in this room.

04
Section 4 The Monthly Question

One question. Specific enough to be useful. Honest enough to be uncomfortable when the answer is no. Write it here and write it at the front of wherever you keep notes so you see it every month.

The Letter Itself
Dear Future Me…

Write it here. In your voice. To the version of you who will open this in 90 days.

Start with: When I graduated from this program, here is what I knew to be true…
🔒
Seal this. Set a reminder for 90 days from today.

When you open it, read it as the person you were in that room. Not who you have become since. Who you were when the decisions were fresh and the commitment was clear.
