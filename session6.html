Who Am I Now

This session does not teach anything new. It integrates everything you have built. You arrived knowing you were stuck. You leave knowing exactly who you are, who you serve, how you reach them, and how you lead. Today you say that out loud in front of the group.

01Session Framework
The Reckoning
You Are Not Discovering Something. You Are Saying It Out Loud.
The Hot Seat is not diagnostic. It is revelatory. Every participant stands and answers four questions in front of the group. How you were leading before. The pattern that kept you from growing. How you will show up now. Whether you have the tools and the belief to build what you described.
The Declaration
The Group Witnesses It. That Is What Makes It Real.
Every practitioner names their pattern in front of the cohort. The group commits to calling it out if they see it resurface. This is not accountability theater. It is the moment a private insight becomes a public commitment held by people who were in the room when the decision was made.
The Launch
The Program Ends. The Foundation Does Not.
The 90 Day Compass and the Letter to Your Future Self are the tools that keep you operating from the foundation when the program is no longer holding you accountable. Three priorities. One monthly question. Day 30, 60, and 90 check ins. The compass is in your hands now.
