PART 2 The Hot Seat

Four questions. Answer from the truth. The hot seat in Session 6 is not diagnostic. It is revelatory. You are not discovering something new. You are putting into words something you already know.

01How were you leading your business before this program?
Be specific. What decisions were you avoiding? What were you building toward and why?
02What patterns did you see about yourself that kept you from growing?
Name it directly. The more specific you are, the more useful this becomes for you and the group.
03How will you show up now?
Not who you want to be. Who you are deciding to be. What decisions will you make differently?
04Are you confident you have the tools and the belief to build the future you described?
A genuine yes carries weight. A hesitation carries even more. Be honest.
