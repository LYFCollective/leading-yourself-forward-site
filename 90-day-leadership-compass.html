90-Day Leadership
Compass

The program is complete. The leadership is not.

What you built in this program is not a completed project. It is a foundation. The 90-day compass is the tool that keeps you operating from that foundation when the program is no longer holding you accountable. Keep it visible. Return to it monthly. Let it do its job.

Part 1 Your Identity Foundation

The decisions you leave with. Write the final versions here. Not drafts. Not intentions. The decisions as they stand today.

Part 2 The Pattern I Am Watching

Named publicly today. Held by the group. Write it here so you see it every time you open this document. The pattern does not disappear. It becomes manageable when you know its name and when others know it too.

Part 3 The 90-Day Leadership Compass

Three priorities. No renegotiation until day 91. These are not tasks. They are decisions about how you lead.

Priority 1 — Who I am leading as
Priority 2 — Who I am building for
Priority 3 — How I will stay on course
Part 4 The Monthly Check-In

One question. Asked every 30 days. Am I making decisions from my identity and my values, or am I making decisions from fear and habit?

Part 5 Check-In Dates

Set three calendar reminders right now. At each one: open this document, read your letter, answer your monthly question, and write one honest sentence about where you are.

Day 30 Check-In
Day 60 Check-In
Day 90 Check-In
