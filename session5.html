01Session Framework
The Principle
The Container Serves the Avatar. Not the Practitioner.
Every decision about how your work is delivered should be filtered through one question: does this make it easier for the right person to say yes and experience transformation? Format, duration, price, sequence, access point — all of it flows from the avatar and the lane, not from what is convenient or familiar.
The Work
Your Journey Archetype Is Already There in the Data.
How your ideal client moves toward a yes is not random. It follows a pattern that matches who they are. Your archetype is the one that fits that pattern — not the one you would prefer to use, not the one that seems most scalable. The work of this session is to identify the match and build from it.
The Commitment
One Container. Fully Built. Before Anything Else.
The practitioner with three half built containers has nothing. The practitioner with one container that passes all four alignment tests has a business. Session 5 ends with one container decided. The bridge puts it into the world once before Session 6.
