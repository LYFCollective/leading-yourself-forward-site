Being stuck is not a failure. It is a signal that leadership is required. You did not get stuck because you are not capable. You got stuck because something needs a decision and the decision has been waiting. Today we find it.
PART 1 Name the Stuck
STEP 1A

Name the Stuck

Describe your current stuck state as specifically as you can. Not "I feel overwhelmed." What exactly is the situation, the decision, or the loop you are in?

STEP 1B

Separate Emotion from Strategy

Two columns. Left: what you feel about this situation. Right: what is actually true about it.

STEP 1C

Find the Decision

Underneath every stuck is an unmade decision. Look at what is actually true (right column above). What decision is sitting there waiting to be made?

PART 2 Your Leadership Pattern

This is the pattern you fall into when stuck. Naming it is the first act of self leadership.

PART 3 The One Thing

From everything on your plate right now: what is the ONE thing that, if you got clear on it, would make the most difference? Not the most urgent. Not the one you feel guilty about. The one that would actually move things forward.
